## What Are Cookies?

### Definition

**Cookies** are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, smartphone, tablet) when you visit a website or use an application. They help the service "remember" information about your visit and preferences.

### Similar Technologies

In addition to cookies, we may use:

- **Local Storage**: HTML5 local storage and session storage
- **IndexedDB**: Local database storage in web browsers
- **SharedPreferences** (Android) / **UserDefaults** (iOS): Local data storage in mobile apps
- **Cache**: Temporary storage of data for faster access
- **Tracking Pixels / Web Beacons**: Invisible images used for analytics
- **SDKs**: Software Development Kits that collect usage data
- **Device Identifiers**: Unique identifiers assigned to your device

For simplicity, we refer to all these technologies collectively as "cookies" in this policy.

## How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

### 1. Essential / Strictly Necessary Cookies

**Purpose**: Enable basic functionality of our Services  
**Can be disabled**: NO - These are required for the app to function  

**What they do**:
- Authenticate your account login
- Remember your IPTV provider credentials (encrypted)
- Maintain your session while using the app
- Store security tokens and API keys
- Enable core features like video playback
- Remember your language preferences

**Examples**:
- `session_id` - Maintains your active session
- `auth_token` - Validates your authentication
- `device_id` - Identifies your device for service delivery

---

### 2. Functionality / Preference Cookies

**Purpose**: Remember your preferences and settings  
**Can be disabled**: YES - Via app settings  

**What they do**:
- Save your favorite channels and content
- Remember your preferred video quality settings
- Store your playback position (continue watching)
- Remember parental control settings
- Store your theme preference (dark/light mode)
- Remember recently viewed channels
- Save subtitle and audio track preferences
- Store your preferred video player settings

**Examples**:
- `favorites_list` - Your saved favorite channels
- `video_quality` - Preferred streaming quality
- `theme_mode` - Dark or light theme preference
- `parental_pin` - Encrypted parental control PIN

### 3. Analytics / Performance Cookies

**Purpose**: Understand how users interact with our Services  
**Can be disabled**: YES - Via app settings  

**What they do**:
- Track which features are most used
- Measure app performance and loading times
- Identify crashes and technical errors
- Analyze user navigation patterns
- Count number of visits and sessions
- Understand which content is popular

**Third-party services we use**:

**You can opt-out** of analytics tracking in **Settings > Privacy > Analytics**.

## How Long Do Cookies Last?

### Session Cookies
- **Duration**: Deleted when you close the app or browser
- **Purpose**: Temporary functionality during your visit
- **Examples**: Session ID, temporary authentication tokens

### Persistent Cookies
- **Duration**: Remain on your device until:
  - You manually delete them
  - They reach their expiration date (ranging from days to years)
  - You uninstall the app
- **Purpose**: Remember your preferences between sessions
- **Examples**: Login credentials, favorites, settings

## Managing Cookies

### In the NeXtv App

You can control cookies through the app settings:

**iOS/Android App**:
1. Open NeXtv
2. Go to **Settings** > **Privacy**
3. Toggle options:
   - **Remember Login**: Keep me signed in
   - **Save Watch History**: Track what I watch
   - **Analytics**: Share usage data
   - **Personalized Ads**: Show relevant ads (if applicable)
   - **Crash Reports**: Send error reports
4. Tap **Clear All Data** to delete all locally stored information

**Web App**:
1. Visit the NeXtv web app
2. Click **Settings** (gear icon)
3. Navigate to **Privacy & Cookies**
4. Adjust your preferences
5. Click **Clear Cookies** to remove all cookies

### In Your Browser (Web Version)

**Google Chrome**:
1. Settings > Privacy and security > Cookies and other site data
2. Choose "Block third-party cookies" or "Block all cookies"
3. Manage exceptions for specific sites

**Safari**:
1. Preferences > Privacy
2. Choose "Block all cookies" or "Prevent cross-site tracking"

**Firefox**:
1. Options > Privacy & Security
2. Enhanced Tracking Protection: choose "Strict"
3. Cookies and Site Data: manage or clear

**Microsoft Edge**:
1. Settings > Cookies and site permissions
2. Manage and delete cookies

### On Your Mobile Device

**iOS**:
1. Settings > Privacy > Tracking
2. Toggle "Allow Apps to Request to Track" OFF
3. Settings > Privacy > Apple Advertising
4. Toggle "Personalized Ads" OFF

**Android**:
1. Settings > Google > Ads
2. Toggle "Opt out of Ads Personalization"
3. Settings > Privacy > Advanced > Ads
4. Reset advertising ID
