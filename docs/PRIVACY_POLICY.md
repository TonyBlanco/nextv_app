## Information We Collect

### Personal Information You Provide

We may collect the following information that you provide directly to us:

- **Account Information**: Username, email address, and password when you create an account
- **IPTV Provider Credentials**: Server URL, username, and password for your IPTV service provider(s)

- **Contact Information**: Email address for customer support and communications
- **User Preferences**: Language settings, favorite channels, parental control settings, and other customization preferences

### Information Collected Automatically

When you use our Services, we may automatically collect certain information:

- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers, mobile network information
- **Usage Data**: Features used, content accessed, viewing history, search queries, interaction with the app
- **Log Data**: IP address, crash logs, system activity, date and time stamps, referral URLs
- **Location Information**: Approximate location based on IP address (we do not collect precise GPS location)
- **Performance Data**: App performance metrics, error reports, diagnostic information

### Information from Third Parties

- **IPTV Provider Data**: EPG (Electronic Program Guide) information, channel lists, and content metadata from your configured IPTV providers
- **Analytics Services**: Anonymous usage statistics and crash reports from third-party analytics tools

## Data Storage and Security

### Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

- **Encryption**: IPTV provider credentials are encrypted using AES-256 encryption before storage
- **Secure Transmission**: All data transmitted between the app and our servers uses TLS/SSL encryption
- **Access Controls**: Restricted access to personal information on a need-to-know basis
- **Regular Security Audits**: Periodic security assessments and vulnerability testing
- **Secure Development Practices**: Following OWASP guidelines and secure coding standards

### Data Storage

- **Local Storage**: IPTV credentials, favorites, and preferences are stored locally on your device using encrypted storage (SharedPreferences with encryption, Hive encrypted boxes)
- **Cloud Storage**: Minimal personal information (account details, usage statistics) is stored on secure cloud servers
- **Data Retention**: We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide our Services and comply with legal obligations

### Limitations

While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.
