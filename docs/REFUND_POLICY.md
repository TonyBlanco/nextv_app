## Platform-Specific Refund Policies

Since NeXtv does not directly process payments, all refund requests must be submitted through the platform where you made your purchase.

### Apple App Store (iOS / macOS)

#### How to Request a Refund

1. **Via Apple Website**:
   - Visit: https://reportaproblem.apple.com/
   - Sign in with your Apple ID
   - Find the NeXtv subscription/purchase
   - Click "Report a Problem"
   - Select "Request a refund"
   - Choose a reason and submit

2. **Via iPhone/iPad**:
   - Open the **App Store** app
   - Tap your **profile icon** (top right)
   - Tap **Purchase History**
   - Find the NeXtv transaction
   - Tap **Report a Problem**
   - Follow the prompts

3. **Contact Apple Support**:
   - Visit: https://support.apple.com/
   - Call Apple Support directly
   - Explain your refund request

#### Apple's Refund Policy

- **Eligibility**: Determined by Apple on a case-by-case basis
- **Timeframe**: Typically within 90 days of purchase
- **Approval**: Not guaranteed; Apple has sole discretion
- **Processing Time**: 2-7 business days if approved
- **Method**: Refunded to original payment method

#### More Information
- Apple Support: https://support.apple.com/billing
- Apple Refund Policy: https://support.apple.com/en-us/HT204084

---

### Google Play Store (Android)

#### How to Request a Refund

1. **Within 48 Hours** (Automatic):
   - Visit: https://play.google.com/store/account/orderhistory
   - Find the NeXtv purchase
   - Click **Request a refund** or **Report a problem**
   - Select a reason
   - Click **Submit**

2. **After 48 Hours**:
   - Open **Google Play Store** app
   - Tap **Menu** > **Subscriptions**
   - Find NeXtv subscription
   - Tap **Manage**
   - Select **Cancel** or contact support
   - For refunds beyond 48 hours, contact Google Play support directly

3. **Contact Google Support**:
   - Visit: https://support.google.com/googleplay/
   - Click **Contact Us**
   - Select **Subscriptions & payments**
   - Choose your issue and submit a request

#### Google's Refund Policy

- **Within 48 hours**: Eligible for refund
- **After 48 hours**: Case-by-case basis; contact support
- **Subscriptions**: Pro-rated refunds may be available
- **Processing Time**: 1-4 business days
- **Method**: Refunded to original payment method

#### More Information
- Google Play Support: https://support.google.com/googleplay/
- Refund Policy: https://support.google.com/googleplay/answer/2479637

## Subscription Management

### How to Cancel Your Subscription

**IMPORTANT**: Uninstalling the app does NOT cancel your subscription. You must cancel through the platform where you subscribed.

#### iOS / macOS (Apple)

1. Open **Settings** on your iPhone/iPad
2. Tap your **Apple ID** at the top
3. Tap **Subscriptions**
4. Find **NeXtv** in the list
5. Tap **Cancel Subscription**
6. Confirm cancellation

**Alternative Method**:
- Open **App Store** > **Profile Icon** > **Subscriptions** > Select NeXtv > **Cancel**

#### Android (Google Play)

1. Open **Google Play Store** app
2. Tap your **profile icon** (top right)
3. Tap **Payments & subscriptions** > **Subscriptions**
4. Select **NeXtv**
5. Tap **Cancel subscription**
6. Follow the prompts

#### Windows (Microsoft Store)

1. Go to: https://account.microsoft.com/services
2. Sign in with your Microsoft account
3. Find **NeXtv** subscription
4. Click **Manage**
5. Click **Cancel**

#### What Happens When You Cancel

- ✓ Your subscription remains active until the end of the current billing period
- ✓ You retain access to premium features until expiration
- ✓ Auto-renewal is turned off
- ✓ You won't be charged again
- ✓ You can resubscribe at any time

## Refund Eligibility Grounds

While final decisions are made by the platform, refunds are typically considered for:

### ✅ Valid Refund Reasons

1. **Technical Issues**:
   - App crashes consistently and cannot be fixed
   - Core features don't work as advertised
   - Compatibility issues that prevent usage
   - **Note**: Contact our support first; most issues can be resolved

2. **Accidental Purchase**:
   - Unintended subscription activation
   - Purchase made by mistake
   - Duplicate charges

3. **Unauthorized Purchase**:
   - Purchase made without your permission
   - Fraudulent transaction
   - Child's unauthorized purchase

4. **Service Not as Described**:
   - App doesn't match store description
   - Features or functionality missing
   - False advertising

5. **Billing Errors**:
   - Double charged
   - Charged wrong amount
   - Charged after cancellation

### ❌ Typically NOT Eligible for Refund

1. **IPTV Provider Issues**:
   - Your IPTV service provider is down or slow
   - Content unavailable from your IPTV provider
   - IPTV provider changed their service
   - **Note**: NeXtv is a player app; we don't control IPTV provider services

2. **User Error**:
   - Forgot to cancel before renewal
   - Chose wrong subscription tier
   - Don't use the app after subscribing

3. **Change of Mind**:
   - Simply don't want the subscription anymore
   - Found a different app you prefer
   - **Note**: Some platforms allow change-of-mind refunds within a short window

4. **Internet Connection Issues**:
   - Your ISP is slow or unstable
   - Network problems on your end
   - Bandwidth limitations

5. **Device Limitations**:
   - Your device doesn't meet minimum requirements
   - Storage issues on your device
   - Operating system incompatibility (if clearly stated in requirements)
